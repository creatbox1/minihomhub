Our Commitment to Accessibility

HVAC Cost Guide is committed to ensuring that our website and all calculators, tools, and content are accessible to all users, including individuals with disabilities. We believe that accessibility is a fundamental right, and we strive to provide an inclusive experience for everyone regardless of ability.

For detailed guides and tips on HVAC systems, read our HVAC blog articles.

We are committed to meeting the standards of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA developed by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). These guidelines explain how to make web content accessible to people with a wide array of disabilities and are consistent with other accessibility standards worldwide.

Accessibility Features

We have implemented the following accessibility features on our website:

Keyboard Navigation

  • All interactive elements (links, buttons, form fields, calculator inputs) are accessible via keyboard
  • Visible focus indicators highlight the currently focused element
  • Skip-to-content link allows keyboard users to bypass navigation
  • Logical tab order follows the visual layout of each page

Screen Reader Compatibility

  • Semantic HTML structure with proper heading hierarchy (H1, H2, H3, etc.)
  • Descriptive alt text for all informative images and icons
  • ARIA labels and roles where necessary to clarify element purpose
  • Form labels are properly associated with their input fields
  • Calculator results are announced to screen readers when updated

Visual Accessibility

  • High color contrast ratios (minimum 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text)
  • Text is resizable without loss of content or functionality
  • Content does not depend on color alone to convey information
  • Responsive design adapts to different screen sizes and zoom levels

Content Accessibility

  • Clear, plain language avoids unnecessary jargon
  • Tables have proper header associations
  • Forms provide clear error messages and input validation
  • Calculator instructions are concise and actionable

Known Limitations

While we strive to make our website fully accessible, we acknowledge that some areas may have limitations. We are actively working to address these:

  • Complex Charts: Some data visualizations (Chart.js charts) may not be fully accessible to screen readers. We are working to provide text-based alternatives for all charts.
  • Older Content: Some older articles may not have complete alt text for all images. We are auditing and updating existing content.
  • PDF Documents: If we publish PDFs in the future, we will ensure they are tagged and accessible.

If you encounter any accessibility barriers on our website, please contact us and we will work to resolve the issue promptly.

Technologies Used

Our website is built using the following technologies, which we have selected for their accessibility support:

  • HTML5: Semantic markup with proper ARIA attributes
  • CSS3: Responsive design with high-contrast color palette
  • JavaScript (ES6+): Progressive enhancement with accessible event handlers
  • Chart.js: Data visualization with configurable accessibility options

Our Assessment Approach

We assess the accessibility of our website through:

  • Automated Testing: Regular scans using tools such as WAVE, Lighthouse, and axe DevTools
  • Manual Testing: Keyboard-only navigation testing and screen reader testing with NVDA, JAWS, and VoiceOver
  • User Feedback: We welcome feedback from users with disabilities to identify and address accessibility barriers
  • Ongoing Training: Our development and content teams receive regular training on accessibility best practices

Conformance Status

HVAC Cost Guide is partially conformant with WCAG 2.1 Level AA. "Partially conformant" means that some parts of the content do not fully conform to the accessibility standard. We are actively working to achieve full conformance and expect to make significant progress within the next 12 months.

Reporting Accessibility Barriers

If you encounter accessibility barriers on our website or have suggestions for improvement, please let us know. We value your feedback and will work to address the issue:

  • Email: support@minihomehub.online
  • Contact Form: minihomehub.online/contact.html
  • Response Time: We will acknowledge your message within 48 business hours and work to resolve the issue within 10 business days.

Enforcement

If you are not satisfied with our response to your accessibility concern, you may escalate your complaint to the relevant enforcement authority in your jurisdiction. In the United States, you may contact the U.S. Department of Justice Civil Rights Division. In Canada, you may contact the Canadian Human Rights Commission.

Future Accessibility Plans

We are committed to continuous improvement. Our upcoming accessibility initiatives include:

  • Providing text-based data tables as alternatives to all charts and graphs
  • Conducting a full third-party accessibility audit
  • Adding an accessibility statement widget to every page
  • Implementing user-controlled text sizing and contrast adjustment options
  • Publishing a quarterly accessibility progress report
