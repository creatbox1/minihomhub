Keep your HVAC system running at peak performance with tools that analyze maintenance plan value, refrigerant upgrade costs, and repair-versus-replacement decisions. These calculators help you avoid costly surprises and plan for the long term. For installation and sizing tools, visit IAQ, Load & Sizing. For heat pump-specific cost and sizing calculators, see Heat Pump & Hybrid Focused.

Every HVAC system eventually needs a major decision, and these calculators are designed to help you make those calls with confidence rather than panic. The Maintenance Plan Cost vs Savings Analyzer compares annual plan fees against average per-visit costs for tune-ups, filter changes, and priority service — most homeowners break even around two to three years, but the tool adjusts for your system's age and local labor rates. The Refrigerant Upgrade Cost Estimator addresses the 2026 R-32 transition head-on: input your current system's refrigerant type and age, and it tells you whether a proactive upgrade or wait-and-see approach makes more financial sense. If you are planning broader home electrification, the Home Electrification Cost Planner goes beyond HVAC to include EV chargers, induction ranges, and solar panels for a complete project budget. And when your system fails on a holiday weekend, the Emergency Repair vs Replacement Calculator removes the emotion by comparing the repair cost against the prorated value and efficiency gains of a new system. Each tool uses up-to-date 2026 pricing data.
