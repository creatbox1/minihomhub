Whether you are sizing a new heat pump, comparing a hybrid dual-fuel system, evaluating mini-split costs, or calculating geothermal payback, these purpose-built tools give you precise, localized estimates. Heat pumps and hybrid systems are the future of home comfort, and these calculators take the guesswork out of your decision. For seasonal projections and regional cost data, visit Seasonal & Regional. For smart thermostat and insulation ROI tools, see Efficiency & Smart Upgrades.

Choosing between a heat pump, a gas furnace, a hybrid system, or geothermal is one of the most consequential HVAC decisions a homeowner can make — the right answer depends on your climate, utility rates, home size, and existing ductwork. The Heat Pump Sizing & Cost Estimator determines the correct capacity using your home's square footage, insulation, and climate zone, then provides installation cost estimates for air-source, ductless, and cold-climate models. The Savings Calculator compares annual operating costs between a heat pump and your current gas furnace or AC using your actual fuel prices for a side-by-side economic analysis. If you are considering a room-by-room approach, the Ductless Mini Split calculator handles multi-head configurations and gives per-zone pricing. For homeowners who want flexibility, the Hybrid Heat Pump + Furnace tool models when the system switches between fuel sources based on outdoor temperature and relative fuel costs. The Geothermal Cost & Payback Calculator factors in loop type, soil conditions, and available tax credits to project long-term returns.
