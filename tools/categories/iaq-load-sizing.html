Proper system sizing and indoor air quality are the foundation of an efficient, comfortable home. Use these tools to perform Manual J load calculations, evaluate duct sealing savings, compare humidity control solutions, and estimate IAQ upgrade costs. For seasonal cost and usage projections, visit Seasonal & Regional. For energy-efficiency upgrades and payback analysis, see Efficiency & Smart Upgrades.

Most HVAC problems trace back to one of two root causes: the system is the wrong size, or indoor air quality is managed reactively instead of proactively. My Whole House HVAC Load Calculator simplifies the Manual J methodology into a straightforward tool — enter your home's dimensions, insulation levels, window types, and climate zone, and it recommends the right capacity in tons or BTUs. Oversizing is actually worse than undersizing in many cases, causing short cycling that wastes energy and shortens equipment life. The IAQ Upgrade Cost & Savings Tool helps you compare solutions across MERV filters, UV-C lights, whole-home dehumidifiers, and ERV/HRV ventilators, each with installation cost estimates and expected energy impacts. The Duct Sealing Savings Estimator addresses the 20–30% of conditioned air that typical duct systems lose — it calculates your specific leakage cost based on duct location and sealing difficulty. The Humidity Control Cost Impact Calculator shows how managing moisture levels affects both comfort and your cooling bills throughout the year.
