About This Calculator

This heat pump savings calculator compares operating costs between heat pumps and gas furnaces or air conditioners. Enter your home specifications and local utility rates above to get instant estimates. The comparison accounts for furnace efficiency ratings, heat pump COP, and local fuel costs to provide accurate projections.

According to the U.S. Department of Energy, heat pumps can reduce electricity use by up to 50% compared to electric resistance heating. For a deeper comparison, read our guide on heat pump vs gas furnace 2026 costs and savings.

All estimates are for educational and informational purposes only. Actual costs vary by region, system efficiency, and home characteristics.
